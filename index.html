Welcome to InfraWash

At InfraWash, our mission is to revolutionize the way roadway infrastructures are maintained and cleaned. Established with a vision to enhance the cleanliness, safety, and longevity of public and private infrastructure, we specialize in designing, building, and implementing cutting-edge equipment tailored for cleaning tunnel walls, soundwall barriers, and barrier walls.

❮ ❯

Our Mission

InfraWash is not just a name in the industry; it's a testament to our journey from seasoned service providers to pioneers in designing and manufacturing advanced roadway infrastructure cleaning equipment. Born out of years of hands-on experience in operating our own service business, we've encountered firsthand the challenges and necessities of maintaining tunnel walls, soundwall barriers, and barrier walls at their best. This unique perspective is what sets us apart and drives our mission to offer unmatched cleaning solutions.

Our Approach

Our transition from service providers to innovators in cleaning technology is fueled by a deep understanding of the field's demands and the inefficiencies of available equipment. With a small, dedicated team of engineers and industry experts, InfraWash was founded to bridge this gap, applying our real-world experience to create equipment that meets the actual needs of maintenance crews and infrastructure managers.

Innovation in Cleaning

Recognizing that each project has its unique set of requirements, InfraWash offers customizable solutions tailored to the specific needs of our clients. Our commitment to innovation drives us to continuously improve and expand our product line.

  • Tunnel Washing Systems: Designed for precision cleaning, ensuring thorough maintenance with minimal disruption.
  • Soundwall and Barrier Wall Maintenance: Tailored tools that cater to the unique challenges of soundwall and barrier maintenance, promoting longevity and aesthetic appeal.
  • Custom Solutions: Recognizing the diversity of infrastructure maintenance needs, we offer custom engineering services to develop solutions that perfectly match your project requirements.
